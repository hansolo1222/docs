## Welcome to BetterWaifu

BetterWaifu is a powerful AI image generation platform that turns your text descriptions into beautiful NSFW and SFW images.

## Quick Start Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up for BetterWaifu to start generating images. We offer various subscription tiers to fit your needs.
    <a href="/subscribing">Learn more about our subscription options</a>
  </Step>
    <Step title="Choose your model">
    Select the best model for your purpose.
        <a href="/prompting/models/intro-to-models">Learn about AI models</a>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Write a prompt">
    Describe the image you want to create. The more detailed your prompt, the better the results.
    <a href="/prompting/basics">Learn how to write effective prompts</a>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate">
    Click the generate button and watch the magic happen!
  </Step>
</Steps>
