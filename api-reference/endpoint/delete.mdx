## Deleting Images

Use this endpoint to permanently delete images from your account.

### Path Parameters

- `id` (required) - The unique identifier of the image to delete

### Response

Returns a confirmation of the deletion operation.

### Notes

- Deleted images cannot be recovered
- You can only delete images that belong to your account
- Deletion frees up storage quota for your account
